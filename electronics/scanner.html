3D Scanner

Work in progress for a 3D scanner based on line laser, a webcam a rotating table.


Hardware


Fig. 1 - Adjustable structure based on wood beams and 3d printed parts to support the components.

Software


Fig. 2 - Capture software, connects to the webcam and arduino(via serial port), to control the laser and stepper driver.

Results


Fig. 3 - Output of the 3d scan, the points in 3D space and with the captured color.
